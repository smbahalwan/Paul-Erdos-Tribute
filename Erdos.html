Paul Erdos

The Man who loved only Numbers

A time line of Erdos' life

  • 1913 - Born in Budapest, Austria-Hungary
  • 1930 - Studied for his doctorate at the University Pazmany Peter in Budapest
  • 1934 - Awarded a doctorate in Mathematics from the University of Budapest
  • 1934 - Moved to Manchester, England, to be a guest lecturer
  • 1935 - Visited Cambridge
  • 1938 - Accepted the position as a scholarship holder at Princeton University
  • 1943 - Worked at Purdue University
  • 1948 - Returned to Hungary
  • 1951 - Received Cole Prize of the American Mathematical Society
  • 1953 - Spent academic years at the University of Notre Dame, Indiana
  • 1983 - Won Wolf Prize
  • 1996 - Died in Warsaw, Poland
